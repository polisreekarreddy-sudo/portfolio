Sundar is the CEO of Google and Alphabet and serves on Alphabet's Board of Directors. Under his leadership, Google has been focused on developing products and services, powered by the latest advances in AI, that offer help in moments big and small.

Sundar Pichai's success story is a remarkable journey from a modest background in India to becoming the CEO of Google and Alphabet Inc. He rose through the ranks at Google, leading product management and innovation for several key products like Google Chrome and Chrome OS. His leadership skills and strategic thinking led him to the top, showcasing the power of perseverance and adaptability.
